Programme Area (Level 5 Diploma)

GENERAL ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

Candidates must fulfil requirements A, B & C

a. minimum general requirement

  1. Minimum FIVE (5) credits* in GCE 'O' Level or its equivalent qualification including:
    1. At least a credit C6 in GCE 'O' Level English OR
      a grade 'B' in IGCSE English as a Second Language OR
      an IELTS score of 6.0*
    2. At least a credit C6 in Mathematics D OR Additional Mathematics
    3. At least a credit C6 in THREE (3) other relevant subjects
  2. Obtained between 5 to 30 points

*Minimum credit accumulation does not include non-English medium subjects.
*A credit in Bahasa Melayu is required for the Brunei Government Scholarship.
*A higher IELTS score of 6.5 is required for the Brunei Government Scholarship


b. minimum requirement for each programme.

c. pass the interview and/or entrance test conducted by politeknik brunei where applicable.

d. must clear criminal record screening.
